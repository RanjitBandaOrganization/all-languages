Elasticity is the ability to quickly increase
or decrease computer processing and resources.

=	=	=	=	=	=	=	=	=
Elasticity goes hand in hand with scalability
and describes the ability to quickly expand
or decrease computer processing,
memory, and storage resources to meet changing demands,
without worrying about capacity planning
and engineering for peak usage.


You can say that elastic computing
is what enables scalability.
